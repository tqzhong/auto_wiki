# Andrej Karpathy

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is a Slovak-Canadian AI researcher, educator, and software engineer known for his work in deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. He is widely regarded as one of the best communicators in the AI field.

## Key Facts

- **Former Director of AI at Tesla** (2017–2022): Led the Autopilot vision team
- **Co-founder of OpenAI**: Early team member
- **Stanford PhD**: Studied under Fei-Fei Li, focused on deep learning for vision and language
- **CS231n**: Created the legendary Stanford course on Convolutional Neural Networks
- **karpathy.ai / Eureka Labs**: Founded an AI-native education company
- **Prolific educator**: Known for clear, accessible explanations of complex AI concepts

## Contributions to Knowledge Management

- Proposed the **[LLM Wiki Pattern](../concepts/llm-wiki-pattern.md)** — a framework for LLM-maintained personal knowledge bases
- Published the foundational [LLM Wiki Gist](../sources/karpathy-llm-wiki-gist.md) describing the architecture, operations, and philosophy
- The pattern has inspired hundreds of implementations and forks on GitHub

## Notable Works

- **"The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks"** (2015) — classic blog post
- **micrograd** — tiny autograd engine for educational purposes
- **minGPT / nanoGPT** — minimal implementations of GPT for learning
- **llm.c** — efficient LLM training in pure C
- **LLM Wiki Gist** — the pattern behind this wiki

## Connections

- Creator of the [LLM Wiki Pattern](../concepts/llm-wiki-pattern.md) that this wiki implements
